2001-02 NEW JERSEY NETS

Brief History

The New Jersey Nets were a team that just last season had a 26-56 record. With acquiring all-star guard Jason Kidd, acquiring Richard Jefferson from a trade, Kerry Kittles, Kenyon Martin, and Keith Van Horn, the Nets propelled themselves to win the Eastern Conference finals as the first seed. They lost to the Lakers in a sweep in the finals.
