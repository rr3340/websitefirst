THE NETS LEGENDS OF BROOKLYN AND NEW JERSEY

Brief History

The Brooklyn Nets are an organization based in Barclay's, Brooklyn. A team which originated in New Jersey, joined the NBA from the ABA. Home to legends like Jason Kidd, Julius Erving, Kevin Durant, and so much more; This team has been at the center of New Yorker and New Jersey culture for decades. It has gone through several cycles of rebuilding, with its most notable being the victory of its ABA championship, the Jason Kidd era with notable players like Vince Carter, Richard Jefferson, Kenyon Martin Sr., etc.. And the Kyrie-Kevin Durant-James Harden big 3, a team which fizzled out over time due to injuries and front office mismanagement. Currently, Mikal Bridges leads the team. As a younger, promising core, the Brooklyn Nets hope to rebuild and continue contending within the league.
