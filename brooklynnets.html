2020-21 NEW JERSEY NETS

Brief History

The Brooklyn Nets after the 2020-21 season, finally had their stars Kyrie Irving and Kevin Durant on the squad. A young and invigorated franchise, after the partial tearing of point guard Spencer Dinwiddie's ACL, the franchise had decided to trade for superstar James Harden from the Houston Rockets. This set the Nets to be the strongest team in the NBA to that point. The team contended hard, but due to Kyrie's sprain ankle and an aggravated hamstring from James Harden, they fell to the eventual championship Milwuakee Bucks in 7.
